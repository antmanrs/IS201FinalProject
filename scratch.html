Star Wars: A Fan's Universe

The Star Wars franchise has always been a source a joy and excitement over the course of my life.

My Favorite Movies/Shows

  1. The Clone Wars
    • A seven season series detailing the Clone wars, a galactic-wide conflict between the Clone Trooper armies of the Republic and the Droid armies of the CIS.
    • There are many battles shown between the Clones and Droids, with fantastic action scenes and leaves anyone at the edge of their seat.
    • The Clones are great examples of honor and self-sacrifice, putting others' safety and wellbeing above their own lives.
  2. Andor
    • Follows the story of Cassian Andor, a scoundrel that finds himself aligned with the Rebel Alliance in fighting the Empire.
    • The plot it beautifully developed, fitting well into the overall star wars universe.
  3. Rogue One
    • A band of rebels seek to fight the Empire by obtaining the plans of a super weapon, hopeing to find a weakness.
    • This was a fast-paced, action-packed movie that kept its audience wanting more, hence the Andor spin-off show.
    • The heroic sacrifices of many central characters makes this story meaningful and impactful.
  4. Revenge of the Sith
    • Not much needs to be said about this film. Its legendary nature is uncontested by members of the Star Wars community.
    • The mixture of live-action Clone battles, beautiful lightsaber choreography, and a thrilling and heart-wrenching story makes this tale one for the books.
    • Of all of the movies, I have probably rewatched this one the most.
  5. Clone Wars Micro-Series
    • This one is not as well known, but avid Star Wars enthusiasists hold this show in high regard.
    • The micro-series includes short episodes full of action-packed battles and adventures.
    • It adds depth to the stories of well-known characters, like Anakin Skywalker, as well as giving more screen-time to beloved Clones.
